BIOGRAPHY

Asst.Prof. Amalia Melegrito Castro -Born in Pura,Tarlac on May of 1965 to Mariano Melegrito Sr. and Matilde Ramos; youngest of their 7 children. Schooled in Tarlac until First year High School and graduated at the Ramon Magsaysay High School here in Manila. In 1985, she graduated with the degree of Bachelor of Science in Education Major in Filipino at the College of Education in the Royal and Pontifical University of Santo Tomas. Had her M.A. in Filipino at Manuel L.Quezon University in 2006. Initially taught high school in the provinces of Cavite and then in Tarlac. Since 1993 she became a faculty member of University of Santo Tomas. She had almost exclusively taught at the College of Science and had just recently done so in the College of Architecture and College of Fine Arts and Design. She is affiliated with the Department of Filipino in the College of Education.
