BIOGRAPHY

Kevin E. Carpio, MSc, RND is an Instructor III at the Department of Nutrition and Dietetics of the University of Santo Tomas, where he handles professional nutrition courses such as Nutrition Therapy, Food Service Systems, and Applied Wellness and Sports Nutrition.
Mr. Carpio earned his Bachelor’s degree in Nutrition and Dietetics at UST, where he received several awards including 4th place in the Board Licensure Examinations for Nutritionist-Dietitians. He obtained his Master’s degree in Clinical Nutrition at the Philippine Women’s University. Prior to joining the academe, Mr. Carpio was a Therapeutic Dietitian, specializing in hospital dietetics and food service administration. He also worked in the health and wellness industry where he was honed in weight management, sports nutrition, and medical nutrition therapy.
